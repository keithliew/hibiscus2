Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

1. What This Policy Covers

This policy explains how hibiscus2.com ("this website") collects and uses information when you browse the site or submit an enquiry about Lexis Hibiscus 2, Port Dickson. This website is operated by an authorised real estate negotiator marketing the project — it is not the developer's official website.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit the enquiry form or contact us on WhatsApp, we collect: your name, WhatsApp number, buyer type (e.g. Malaysia Buyer, Singapore Buyer, Overseas Buyer, MM2H Participant), and any message you provide. Form submissions are processed via Web3Forms and delivered to our consultant by email.

We also automatically collect standard analytics data (pages visited, device type, approximate location, referring source and, where present, UTM/gclid campaign parameters) via Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics (GA4).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry — to contact you on WhatsApp or by phone/email with villa details, pricing, and to arrange a consultation. Analytics data is used to understand site performance and to measure the effectiveness of Google Ads campaigns (conversion tracking).

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics (GA4) and Google Ads conversion tracking, which may set cookies or similar technologies in your browser to measure site usage and ad performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Sharing of Information

Your enquiry details are shared only with the consultant/agency handling your enquiry and, where relevant, the project developer for the purpose of following up on your interest. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry and for legitimate business record-keeping, unless you request deletion sooner.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us via WhatsApp or the details on our Contact page, in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to us via WhatsApp.
