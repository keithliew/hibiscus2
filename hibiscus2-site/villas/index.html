Effortless Ownership

Ownership, Without the Upkeep

Your villa is furnished, maintained and operated by Lexis Hotel Group under a structured 15-year rental programme — a fixed rental phase for the first three years, followed by a profit-sharing phase for the remaining twelve. You enjoy the resort and the rental income; the operator handles everything else.
