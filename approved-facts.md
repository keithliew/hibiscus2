## HOW TO READ THIS FILE

Each fact carries two flags:

- **Domain flag** — where the fact may be published:
  - `[BOTH]` — allowed on hibiscus2.com AND grrmalaysia.com
  - `[GRR-ONLY]` — allowed ONLY on grrmalaysia.com (organic site). These are
    the return figures, projections and finance-package details that must
    NEVER appear on the ads domain.
- **Status flag**:
  - `[OK]` — stated in the developer sales kit / brochure in this project;
    safe to use as written, cite "developer sales kit" where relevant
  - `[VERIFY]` — appears in draft copy or needs current confirmation from
    Summerfield/developer before publishing. Treat as placeholder until the
    box is ticked.
